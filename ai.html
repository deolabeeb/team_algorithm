Artificial Intelligence

This course provides an in-depth understanding of AI technologies, from foundational algorithms to cutting-edge machine learning techniques.

Milestones

Milestone 1: Introduction to AI

Learn the fundamentals of AI, including key concepts like supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and neural networks.

Milestone 2: Search Algorithms in AI

Understand search algorithms like A* and breadth-first search, and their role in problem-solving.

Milestone 3: Reinforcement Learning

Explore reinforcement learning and create models that learn through interaction with their environment.

Milestone 4: Neural Networks and Deep Learning

Delve into the architecture of neural networks and understand how deep learning models solve complex tasks.

Milestone 5: AI in Robotics

Learn how AI is integrated into robotics to create autonomous systems that can perceive, reason, and act.

Projects

Project 1: Develop a Chatbot

Build a chatbot that utilizes natural language processing (NLP) to simulate intelligent conversations.

Project 2: AI for Image Classification

Create a computer vision system that classifies images using neural networks and machine learning techniques.

Project 3: Reinforcement Learning for Game AI

Build a reinforcement learning model to develop an autonomous agent for game AI.

Certification

Upon successful completion of the Artificial Intelligence course, students will receive a certificate recognizing their expertise in AI technologies. Certification requirements include:

  • Completing all milestones and coursework
  • Submitting and passing all projects
  • Passing the final comprehensive assessment

This certification validates your skills and can significantly boost your career prospects in AI-related fields.
